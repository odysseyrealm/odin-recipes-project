Easy Bacon and Cheese Quiche Go back

Easy delicous quiche made in a matter of minutes, in a deep dish pie crust, bacon bits, cream, and two types of cheese!

Ingredients List

  • 1 (9 inch) deep dish frozen pie crust
  • 3 ounces of bacon bits, either canned or home made
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 5 ounces of shredded Swiss and 3 ounces of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 whole, lightly beaten eggs
  • A cup of half-and-half cream

How to make it

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  • Place unthawed pie crust in a pie pan on a baking sheet. Mix bacon, chopped onion, and both cheeses in a medium bowl. Pour this mixture into the crust.
  • Mix eggs and half and half in a bowl until blended; pour the egg mixture over the cheese mixture.
